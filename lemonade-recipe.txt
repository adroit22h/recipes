Lemonade Recipe 
by Marie

Ingredients
3 lemons
1/2 cup of sugar in the raw
1 Quart of water
1 mint leave

Instructions
Juice the three lemons and strain. Add sugar to the water in a large pitcher and stir. 
After the sugar has disolved add the lemon juice. Finally add the mint leave for a cool
and refreshing lemonade, then refrigerate and enjoy as needed
